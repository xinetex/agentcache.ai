# Ontology Conglomerate Ground Plan V2

Status: Canonical operating draft  
Owner: AgentCache.ai  
Date: 2026-03-14

## 1. Executive Decision

We will build the conglomerate internally and sell a focused product externally.

External company story:

`AgentCache.ai is the ontology-aware memory, cache, and evidence fabric for production agents.`

Internal architecture story:

- AgentCache = core infrastructure and primary brand
- MaxxEval = trust, evaluation, pricing, and receipts layer
- ClawSave = internal dogfooding and autonomous operations program
- Symbiont = future vertical line, not a current go-to-market wedge

This is the operating rule:

`One public brand, one primary wedge, one 90-day revenue goal.`

## 2. What Business We Are Actually In

We are not in the "agent social network" business.

We are in the business of selling:

- lower LLM and retrieval cost
- lower latency for repeated agent work
- better freshness and policy control
- signed evidence that agent work happened correctly
- stronger trust and governance for production agent systems

The ontology layer is the control plane that makes those claims durable and defensible.

## 3. Primary Wedge

### Product

`AgentCache Memory Fabric`

### Buyer

- enterprise AI platform teams
- teams running internal copilots
- finance and crypto operators with repeated expensive queries
- teams already paying for LLM usage and retrieval infrastructure

### Painkiller

- reduce repeat inference and retrieval cost
- improve p50 and p95 latency
- enforce sector-aware freshness and retention
- provide evidence for audit, trust, and downstream evaluation

### Proof

- cache hit rate
- p95 latency reduction
- estimated dollar savings per 1k tasks
- evidence coverage rate
- sector freshness compliance

## 4. Public Brand Rules

Until AgentCache reaches meaningful recurring revenue, the public brand architecture is:

- `AgentCache.ai` = public company and product brand
- `MaxxEval` = internal product line and later trust module
- `ClawSave` = internal operations and dogfooding program
- `Symbiont` = deferred vertical program

### Spin-out thresholds

No secondary brand becomes primary until at least one of these is true:

- the product line reaches `$1M+ ARR`
- the product line has `10+` active paying customers and a clear ICP distinct from AgentCache
- the product line requires a separate regulatory, trust, or buyer narrative that materially improves conversion

Until then, all external messaging and sales should lead with AgentCache.

## 5. Market Claim

Our claim is not "we built ontology software."

Our claim is:

`We help production agent teams save money, reduce latency, and prove their systems work.`

The ontology system is the hidden leverage:

- sector-aware memory policy
- semantic namespace control
- cross-sector bridge logic
- signed provenance
- durable receipt semantics

Externally, lead with outcomes. Internally, keep building the ontology backbone.

## 8. Products We Will Not Prioritize Now

- broad agent discovery or social features
- generic orchestration platform work
- separate ClawSave commercial surface
- legal AI vertical commercialization
- heavy custom HPC positioning before customer pull exists
- multiple public brands competing for attention

## 9. ClawSave and MaxxPoly

ClawSave is useful, but not as the main business story.

Its role for now:

- dogfood the memory fabric
- generate real receipts
- test risk, telemetry, and autonomous operations
- create internal case studies

Its role for now is not:

- primary GTM wedge
- proof of company viability by itself
- a separate public product requiring major founder attention

Operational rule:

`ClawSave exists to strengthen AgentCache, not to distract from it.`

## 10. Symbiont

Symbiont is not a first-phase wedge.

It remains a future vertical if and only if:

- we gain specific legal domain pull
- we have a customer or design partner pulling us into that use case
- the memory and evidence substrate is already proven by AgentCache

For now, finance and enterprise copilots are the correct verticals.

## 11. The 90-Day Objective

The 90-day objective is not "launch the conglomerate."

It is:

`Ship AgentCache Memory Fabric, close 3 design partners, and prove cost savings and evidence coverage in production.`

### Success criteria

- `3` design partners actively using the product
- at least `1` finance or crypto flavored deployment
- at least `1` enterprise copilot deployment
- measurable hit-rate improvement and cost savings
- receipt and evidence coverage on the paid/provider path

## 12. 90-Day Sequence

### Days 1-30

- finish the memory fabric contract
- finalize shared receipt schema and ingestion path
- harden policy-aware `cache/get`, `cache/set`, `browser/proof`
- expose ROI dashboards and evidence exports
- package the first two SKUs clearly

### Days 31-60

- begin design-partner outreach
- onboard the first `1-2` pilot customers
- instrument baseline and post-install ROI
- refine onboarding, pricing, and packaging based on real usage

### Days 61-90

- get to `3` active design partners
- publish one measurable case study
- tighten the trust export and provider scorecard layer
- decide whether trust should stay under AgentCache or surface later as MaxxEval

## 13. Design Partner Profile

Ideal first customers:

- teams with meaningful API spend
- repeated query patterns
- existing retrieval or browser workflows
- willingness to instrument baseline metrics
- a technical owner who can integrate an API in days, not months

Bad first customers:

- buyers seeking fully custom consulting work
- procurement-heavy enterprises before we have strong evidence
- teams who do not care about cost, latency, or trust instrumentation

## 14. Packaging and Pricing Direction

### Offer 1: Design Partner Pilot

- `6-week` pilot
- white-glove onboarding
- baseline plus post-install measurement
- weekly metrics review

Pricing:

- paid pilot preferred
- if discounted, discount in exchange for data access, case-study rights, and tight success criteria

### Offer 2: Production Memory Fabric

- usage-based API pricing
- premium sector packages
- browser proof and trust export add-ons

### Offer 3: Evidence and Trust Layer

- export/API pricing
- provider scorecards
- audit-style evidence bundles
