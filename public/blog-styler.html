The Hidden Cost of Reasoning Models

Reasoning models like OpenAI o1 and Moonshot K1 are changing the game. But they come with a hidden tax: latency and cost.

The "Reasoning Tax"

Unlike standard LLMs that predict the next token immediately, reasoning models perform a "Chain of Thought" process before outputting a single character. This can take 10-30 seconds.

// Example of a slow reasoning call
const response = await openai.chat.completions.create({
  model: "o1-preview",
  messages: [{ role: "user", content: "Solve P vs NP" }]
});

The Solution: Neuro-Symbolic Caching

We introduce a new architecture inspired by the Prefrontal Cortex Working Memory (PBWM) model. By caching the reasoning trace, not just the final answer, we can reuse expensive "thought" across similar queries.

  • Working Memory: Fast, active context (Redis).
  • Episodic Memory: Long-term storage of successful patterns (Vector DB).
  • Gating: A cheap "critic" model decides what to cache.

This reduces costs by up to 90% for repetitive complex tasks.
