The Paradox

LLMs often ignore negative constraints like "don't use X" because they focus on the concept of X.

The Protocol

We intercept the output, verify it against the rules, and apply corrections before the user sees it.

Cached Compliance

Once verified, the compliant response is cached. Compliance becomes a deterministic guarantee.
