Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 18, 2025

1. Who We Are

AgentCache.ai is operated by Drgnflai.org, an organization dedicated to AI education and innovation. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our AI caching service.

2. What We Collect

Account Data:

  • Email address (for account and billing)
  • API keys (hashed with SHA-256, never stored in plain text)

Usage Data:

  • API request metadata (timestamps, models, cache hit rates)
  • Performance metrics (latency, error rates)

Cached Content:

  • LLM prompts and responses (encrypted, 7-day TTL)
  • Automatically deleted after retention period
  • Never used for training or shared between users

3. What We Don't Collect

  • Your LLM API keys – You call OpenAI/Anthropic directly
  • Personal data from prompts – Cached content is ephemeral
  • Browsing history – Minimal analytics only

4. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to provide and improve the caching service, process billing, prevent fraud, and send service updates. We never sell your data.

5. Data Security

  • TLS 1.3 encryption in transit
  • AES-256 encryption at rest
  • SOC 2 compliant infrastructure (Vercel, Upstash)
  • API keys hashed with SHA-256

6. Data Retention

  • Cached content: 7 days (default)
  • Account data: While active + 90 days after deletion
  • Usage metrics: 2 years (aggregated)

7. Your Rights

You can access, correct, delete, or export your data. Contact privacy@agentcache.ai to exercise these rights.

8. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

We comply with GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California) regulations. We do not sell personal information. California residents and EU users have additional rights detailed in our full privacy policy.
